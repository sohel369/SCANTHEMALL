How the $1000 Cash Draw Works

The Bingo Multiplier System

01 Registration

Register for free via the My Position page. Your entry is valid for the upcoming month's global cash draw. Ensure your contact details are verified to qualify for the prize payout.

02 The Bingo Multiplier

"More uploads = More tickets."

Every verified image upload on Instagram or other platforms adds a ticket to your profile. Completing specific prize "Bingo Lines" (e.g., uploading for all 4 luxury categories) triggers a **10x Multiplier** on your existing cash draw entries.

03 Winner Selection

Winners are selected via a transparent digital draw. We notify winners directly via Email and SMS. If a winner does not respond within 7 days, a re-draw occurs.
