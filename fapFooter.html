General Participation

How do I participate?

Simply upload an image of a partner's advertisement. Each upload earns you a free entry in your regional prize draw.

What images can I upload?

Selfies next to stationary vehicles with ads, or photos/videos of moving vehicles. Must be original and match your profile.
